About

Takagi-san VN is an open source fan Visual Novel game based from Soichirou Yamamoto's work Karakai jouzu no Takagi-san, Set in the town of Tonoshō, Shodo Island(小豆島 Shōdoshima) located somewhere in modern Japan.

The story is told through the perspective of the main character using a first person narrative. The game uses a traditional text and sprite-based visual novel model.

Takagi-san VN is created using the Ren'Py scripting system. It is the product of an international team of amateur Developers, Writers and Artists and is available free of charge under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike License.
